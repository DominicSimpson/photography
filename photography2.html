Photography

Photo highlights

Click on each thumbnail to bring up a larger image

Over the last twenty years, I have taken thousands of photos as an amateur hobbyist photographer. My camera has followed me all the way far west to California; to Ukraine, at Europe's Eastern fringe; and to the world's most Northernly inhabited outpost, Svalbard, beyond which lies the icy wastes of the North Pole.

The following photos, to which I will be adding more regularly, are highlights of my huge archive. Some, such as the photos of glaciers in Svalbard, have had black-and-white or other effects added to them. Others were taken without any embellishments. Click on each thumbnail, and a larger photo will come up with an added description.

Some more photography by me can be viewed on my Instagram account.

If you would like to use one of these photos in a commercial context, please contact me to arrange a price. The photos are available without the descriptive watermark.
